🏀 The Evolution of Basketball
A vibrant, single-page website that tells the story of basketball — from its invention in a winter gymnasium in 1891 all the way to the fast, global sport we know today. It even includes an interactive quiz to test what you've learned!

🔗 Live Website
https://nasserelshaarani-cpu.github.io/basketball-histoire/

📖 What's Inside
The site walks you through the full history of the game:

The Origins — when, where, how, and why basketball was invented (December 1891, Springfield, Massachusetts, by Dr. James Naismith)
How People Used to Play — peach baskets, no dribbling, and the original 13 rules
The Evolution — backboards, dribbling, the shot clock, and the three-point revolution
Then & Now — a side-by-side look at how dramatically the game has changed
Rules Around the World — how the NBA, FIBA, and NCAA each play and referee differently
🧠 Challenge Yourself — an 8-question interactive quiz with instant scoring
🎨 Features

Bold colour palette inspired by classic NBA teams
Fully responsive — looks great on phone, tablet, and desktop
Interactive quiz that grades you instantly and shows the correct answers
